# Gram-Setu User Guide (English)

## Welcome to Gram-Setu! 🌾

Gram-Setu (Village Bridge) is your AI-powered agricultural assistant. Get instant help with:
- Crop disease identification
- Market prices
- Government schemes
- Irrigation advice

---

## How to Use Gram-Setu

### Step 1: Open the Website

1. Open your phone or computer browser
2. Go to: **https://ure-mvp.streamlit.app**
3. Wait for the page to load

### Step 2: Enter Your Information

On the left sidebar, enter:
- **Your Name**: Enter your full name
- **Village**: Select your village from the dropdown
- **District**: Select your district
- **Language**: Choose English, Hindi, or Marathi

Click **"Save Profile"** button

### Step 3: Ask Your Question

Two ways to ask:

**Option A: Type Your Question**
1. Click in the text box at the bottom
2. Type your question
3. Click **"Send"** button or press Enter

**Option B: Upload Crop Image**
1. Click **"Upload Image"** button
2. Select a photo of your crop from your phone/computer
3. Add a question (optional): "What disease is this?"
4. Click **"Send"** button

### Step 4: Read the Answer

- Wait 5-10 seconds for the response
- The answer will appear in your selected language
- You can ask follow-up questions

## Tips for Best Results

### ✅ DO:
- Ask clear, specific questions
- Upload clear, well-lit crop photos
- Mention your crop name (tomato, wheat, onion, etc.)
- Mention your location for weather/price queries
- Ask one question at a time

### ❌ DON'T:
- Ask about politics, religion, or non-farming topics
- Use abusive or inappropriate language
- Share personal information (phone number, address)
- Ask about banned pesticides (DDT, etc.)

---

## Understanding Responses

### Response Parts:

1. **Main Answer**: Direct answer to your question
2. **Treatment/Solution**: Steps to solve the problem
3. **Additional Info**: Market prices, weather, etc.
4. **Confidence**: How sure the AI is (shown as ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐)

### Confidence Levels:

- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 stars): Very confident - Trust this answer
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 stars): Confident - Good answer
- ⭐⭐⭐ (3 stars): Moderate - Consider consulting expert
- ⭐⭐ (2 stars): Low - Definitely consult expert
- ⭐ (1 star): Very low - Do not rely on this answer

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Content Blocked" Message

**Reason**: Your question contains harmful or off-topic content

**Solution**:
- Rephrase your question
- Focus on farming topics only
- Avoid banned pesticides (DDT, Endosulfan, etc.)
- Avoid politics, religion, finance topics

### Problem: "Image Upload Failed"

**Reason**: Image file is too large or wrong format

**Solution**:
- Use JPG or PNG format only
- Keep image size under 5MB
- Compress image if needed
- Take a new, clearer photo

### Problem: "Service Unavailable"

**Reason**: System is temporarily down or overloaded

**Solution**:
- Wait 2-3 minutes
- Try again
- If problem persists, contact support

### Problem: Slow Response

**Reason**: High traffic or complex query

**Solution**:
- Wait patiently (up to 30 seconds)
- Don't click "Send" multiple times
- Simplify your question if too complex

### Problem: Wrong Language Response

**Reason**: Language setting not saved

**Solution**:
- Check language selection in sidebar
- Click "Save Profile" after changing language
- Refresh the page
